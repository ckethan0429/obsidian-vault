# Regional Startup Ecosystem Korea

Regional startup support in Korea is fragmented across provincial platforms, economic agencies, tourism organizations, culture foundations, and innovation centers. The source list highlights that serious opportunity discovery often requires checking the right regional node rather than only national portals.

## Regional nodes mentioned in the source
- 경기스타트업플랫폼
- 경기도경제과학진흥원
- 경기창조혁신센터
- 부산창조경제혁신센터
- 서울문화재단
- 경기문화재단
- 경기도 일자리재단
- 서울관광재단
- 경기관광공사

## Interpretation
This suggests a practical founder workflow:
1. Check national support portals
2. Check region-specific startup and economic agencies
3. Check sector-specific regional institutions such as tourism and culture
4. Check ecosystem platforms for events, investment, and benchmarks

## Why regionality matters
Eligibility, pilot opportunities, PoC programs, and creator support are often region-bound. A startup that ignores regional institutions may miss easier early opportunities.
