# B2B Sales and Negotiation Strategy

A structured guide to B2B sales execution covering lead generation, qualification, discovery, proposal design, negotiation, closing, onboarding, and account expansion.

## Core sales process
The source frames B2B sales as a seven-stage strategic process:
1. Lead generation
2. Qualification
3. Needs analysis and solution design
4. Proposal and negotiation
5. Closing
6. Onboarding and implementation
7. Retention and expansion

## Key operating idea
The central idea is that B2B sales is not simple persuasion. It is a coordinated process of understanding a buyer's decision system, translating pain into quantified value, and reducing risk across the path to signature and expansion.

## Practical value
This page is especially relevant for consulting, infrastructure sales, GPU projects, private-cloud deals, and other solution-led B2B motions where decision cycles are multi-step and multi-stakeholder.
