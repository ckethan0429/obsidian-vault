# Korean AI Agent Skills

A category for AI-agent capabilities designed around Korean services, platforms, and user workflows rather than generic global APIs.

## Characteristics highlighted by the source
- Localized around Korean public and commercial services
- Structured as reusable skills for coding agents
- Emphasizes practical task completion over abstract demos
- Includes setup, secret-management, and operational guidance

## Potential importance
Localized skill ecosystems can be a major wedge for agent adoption because they connect agents to the high-friction services users already care about.

## Example areas from [[k-skill]]
- Transport and reservation workflows
- Government and public-information lookup
- Shopping and delivery workflows
- Korean-language utilities
- Finance and market data
