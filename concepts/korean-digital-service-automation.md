# Korean Digital Service Automation

This concept covers automating Korean digital services through AI agents, scripts, or skill frameworks. The retrieved source suggests a growing ecosystem of wrappers, skills, and proxies that turn fragmented Korean services into agent-usable workflows.

## What the source implies
- High-value automation opportunities exist in everyday Korean services
- Agent adoption can be accelerated by packaging domain-specific skills
- Reliability depends on upstream policy, authentication flows, and anti-bot measures

## Product angle
For founders and operators, localized automation can become either an internal productivity layer or a customer-facing product wedge.
