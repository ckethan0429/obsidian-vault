# Korean Startup Support Sites

A curated starter map of Korean sites useful for founders, small businesses, creators, and operators looking for grants, public support programs, export support, events, education, and ecosystem intelligence.

## Summary
The source is a practical founder-oriented list of sites someone wishes they had known earlier while doing startups in Korea. It emphasizes discovery efficiency rather than deep evaluation.

## Main buckets
- Government and quasi-government support portals: K-Startup, 기업마당, 소상공인24, 소상공인진흥공단, 고용24, 범부처통합연구지원시스템, SMTECH
- Regional platforms and centers: 경기스타트업플랫폼, 경기도경제과학진흥원, 경기창조혁신센터, 부산창조경제혁신센터
- Sector-specific support: 한국관광산업포탈, 지역 관광센터/관광공사, 한국콘텐츠진흥원, 경기콘텐츠진흥원, 한국디자인진흥원, K-ICT 디바이스랩, AI 허브
- Export, IP, and financing: 코트라, 한국무역협회, 중소벤처기업진흥공단, RIPC, 기술보증기금, 신용보증기금
- Ecosystem discovery and events: 이벤터스, 디캠프, 넥스트유니콘, 혁신의숲

## Representative resources from the source
- K-Startup — startup support and commercialization funding
- 기업마당 — aggregated government and local support notices
- 경기스타트업플랫폼 — region-specific startup support in Gyeonggi
- 디캠프 — investment and education-oriented startup ecosystem node
- 혁신의숲 — startup benchmarking and investment intelligence

## Interpretation
This page is best treated as an entry page into the broader ecosystem, not a ranked recommendation list. The list mixes official support portals, regional agencies, event platforms, and ecosystem intelligence services.
